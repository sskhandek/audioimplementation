How to interpret our visualization

There are three main areas to look at: theme, genre, and correlation

The direction of the bars indicates whether the correlation is negative or positive (if you hover over the bar you're interested it in, it will tell you what correlation it had as well as whether it was negative or positive.) The color of the bars indicates which genre we're talking about, and the center label tells you what the theme the subgraph refers to (in our particular visualization we chose to look the common themes of: money/fame, women, drugs/alcohol, and family.
